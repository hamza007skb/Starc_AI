Ask StarcAI

So what does StarcAI do?
StarcAI helps companies build investor confidence and trust.
What value does StarcAI provide to my company?
Companies use our advanced sentiment analysis and proprietary LLM when writing financial reports and letters to stakeholders to induce desired reactions in its investors, leading to an empirically verified 31% increase in investor confidence and a 22% increment in investment likelihood for StarcAI rewritten text compared to the original company-generated text.
Who is this for?
StarcAI is a B2B product for public corporations and SMEs to improve their investor relations and not just anticipate, but also control how the market reacts to what the company puts out.
How does this work?
StarcAI analyses your text using advanced sentiment analysis, finetuned on 4.9 Billion Tokens from financial reports and letters to stakeholders. It then uses a proprietary LLM to rewrite your text to induce your desired reactions in your investors based on your requirements and business context.
